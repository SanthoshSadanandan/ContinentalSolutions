Vertical & Horizontal Laminar Flow Cabinets

This workspace is an enclosed area designed for protection of personnel and samples from particulate contamination. Air is blown through a HEPA filter, eliminating contamination and/or hazardous particles, in a smooth unidirectional flow. The air flow is either vertical or horizontal based on requirement.These hoods are used in clinical, research and pharmaceutical laboratory for testing and drug development procedures. Units can be portable or fixed, made of various materials such as mild steel, stainless steel and acrylic, and come in a variety of dimensions. Choice of cabinet will depend on space available, application, type of filter and light needed.

Class 100 air quality with features such as

  • chemical-resistant materials
  • filter/flow rate monitoring system
  • locking sliding door
  • Adjustable multi-speed blowers
  • ergonomically designed
  • low noise
