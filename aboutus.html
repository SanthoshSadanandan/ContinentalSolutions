About Us

At Continental Solutions, we strive to offer flexible, end-to end clean environment solutions to support your business needs.

Today's world, powered with technology & innovation, is transforming a wide array of industry verticals such as high-end manufacturing, automotive & aerospace, electrical & electronics, power & thermal energy, space technology, civil & infrastructure and information technology. This advent of industrial expansion and transformation also puts on us the onus of being a responsible organization to create a clean and safe environment to carry out effective and efficient business.

Continental Solutions embarks on over three decades of rich experience of the erstwhile Continental Air Control Systems which was serving esteemed clientele spanning Government Maharatna and Navratna companies, private and public sector companies across above industry segments. We strive to bring the latest in digital technologies into our products for a complete differentiated product experience for our customers.

Backed by a dynamic and competent team with strong customer focus, we are here to help serve your business needs.
